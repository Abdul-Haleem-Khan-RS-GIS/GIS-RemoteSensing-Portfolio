### [2. Employment Density Analysis](./Employment-Density-Analysis-Duebendorf)
* **Project Goal:** To identify "hotspots" where jobs are concentrated.
* **Methodology:**
    * I used **Kernel Density Estimation (KDE)**.
    * This created a smooth heat map showing high-density employment zones, which is useful for transit planning.
* **Key Skills:** Spatial Statistics, Heat Mapping, Raster Analysis.

### [3. Social Infrastructure Accessibility](./Zurich-Daycare-Center-Spatial-Accessibility)
* **Project Goal:** To measure how easily residents can reach essential services (schools, hospitals).
* **Methodology:**
    * I mapped social facility locations against residential areas.
    * I performed a proximity analysis to find gaps in service coverage.
* **Key Skills:** Buffer Analysis, Proximity Analysis, Urban Planning.
